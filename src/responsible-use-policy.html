Responsible Use Policy

Accounts that use our product to market goods and/or services will be immediately terminated. You are ultimately responsible for what you do with our product.

Restrictions

In summary, please abide by these guidelines:

  • DO let the reminder recipient know who you are.
  • DO use our product to let your contacts know of an upcoming appointment.
  • DO NOT use our product for marketing.
  • DO NOT message individuals who have not given consent.
  • DO NOT use our product for soliciting charitable contributions, sending political campaign messages, telemarketing, email marketing, or any form of use restricted by the Federal Communications Commission (FTC).
